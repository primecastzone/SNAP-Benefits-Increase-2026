## What Is SNAP?

The Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program (SNAP), formerly known as Food Stamps, is a federal nutrition assistance program that helps eligible low-income individuals and families purchase food.

Benefits are provided through an Electronic Benefit Transfer (EBT) card and can be used at approved grocery stores nationwide.

## How to Apply

1. Submit application via state SNAP office  
2. Provide required documents  
3. Attend interview  
4. Wait for approval decision

## What SNAP Covers

- Fruits and vegetables  
- Meat and dairy  
- Bread and cereals  
- Snacks and beverages  

Not allowed:
- Alcohol
- Tobacco
- Hot prepared meals
