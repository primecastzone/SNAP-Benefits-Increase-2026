# SNAP Benefits Increase 2026

## Overview

The Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program (SNAP) helps eligible low-income households purchase food. Benefit amounts may change annually based on federal cost-of-living adjustments and program updates.

## Who May Qualify?

- Low-income individuals
- Families with children
- Seniors
- People with disabilities

## How to Apply

1. Contact your state SNAP office.
2. Complete an application.
3. Submit required documents.
4. Attend an interview if requested.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Will SNAP benefits increase in 2026?
Benefit levels are updated periodically based on federal guidelines and economic factors.

### How can I check my SNAP status?
You can check through your state's SNAP portal or local office.
